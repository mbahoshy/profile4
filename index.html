TradePro

tradepro.herokuapp.com

At its heart, the concept of TradePro is simple- it replicates electrical circuits in the web browser. The purpose of this application is to train HVAC (heating and cooling) technicians to identify and diagnose electrical failures.

The website offers video lessons, troubleshooting problems, and tests to train users. User statistics are gathered and used to give personal progress reports. TradePro tracks metrics that allow us to identify individual strengths and weaknesses of a user based on furnace type, part type (eg transformer, relay, etc), and more.
